# Security policy

## Supported versions

Security updates are applied to the **latest published minor** of `vuesip` on [npm](https://www.npmjs.com/package/vuesip). Older lines may not receive backports unless explicitly documented in a release.

## Reporting a vulnerability

**Please do not open a public issue** for undisclosed security problems.

1. **Preferred:** Use [GitHub private vulnerability reporting](https://github.com/ironyh/VueSIP/security/advisories/new) for this repository (if enabled in repo settings).
2. **Alternative:** Contact the maintainers with enough detail to reproduce the issue (affected version, component, and impact). You may use a draft security advisory or a private channel if one is published for the project later.

We aim to acknowledge reports within a few business days and coordinate a fix and disclosure timeline with you.

## Scope

In scope: the **VueSip library** (`vuesip` npm package), this **GitHub repository**, and **official starter templates** in `templates/` when used as documented.

Out of scope: third-party PBX systems, your deployment configuration, or forks not maintained here.

## Safe harbor

We appreciate responsible disclosure and will not pursue legal action against researchers who follow this policy in good faith.
